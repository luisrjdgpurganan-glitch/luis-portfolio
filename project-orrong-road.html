Kitchen Hidden Study Laundry

Project Summary

Precision in a compact space

This custom-designed residential kitchen fit-out was developed to create a highly functional and visually refined kitchen space that integrates premium joinery detailing, modern appliances, and efficient storage solutions. The design features a combination of tall cabinetry, overhead wall cabinets, integrated appliances, drawer systems, and a dedicated desk area, maximising both usability and spatial efficiency within the compact layout.

The joinery specification incorporated white melamine internals paired with Polytec Ligurian Walnut Woodmatt doors and panels, creating a warm and contemporary finish. Stone benchtops and splashbacks were specified to complement the cabinetry and provide a durable, high-end surface treatment. Push-to-open mechanisms, concealed hardware, and integrated underpanels contributed to the clean and minimalist aesthetic.

Premium appliances, including integrated refrigeration, induction cooktop, steam combi oven, pyrolytic oven, and built-in rangehood systems, were incorporated seamlessly into the joinery design to maintain an uninterrupted visual line throughout the kitchen.

Client Requirements

  • Custom kitchen joinery
  • Hidden study and laundry
  • Polytec Ligurian Walnut Woodmatt
  • Stone desk top for study
