Project Summary

Calm meets character

The Newport Residence kitchen project was developed to create a functional and calming central living space tailored to the needs of a growing family. As part of a full house renovation, the design focused on balancing practicality with a refined aesthetic, resulting in a kitchen that feels both welcoming and highly functional for everyday living.

The design approach emphasized clean lines, subtle detailing, and a carefully selected material palette. Warm timber textures from Polytec "Habitat" were paired with the crisp finish of "Blossom White," creating a sophisticated contrast that allows the joinery to stand out gently without overpowering the surrounding space.

To ensure durability and long-term usability, the kitchen incorporated a 20mm builder's range stone benchtop — a hard-wearing and low-maintenance surface suited for a busy family environment while remaining aligned with the project budget.

Client Requirements

  • Polytec Habitat used for colour
  • Polytec Blossom White cabinetry
  • 20mm stone benchtops
  • Durable pre-finished surfaces
  • Budget-conscious material selection
  • Integrated kitchen storage solutions
  • High-traffic family functionality
