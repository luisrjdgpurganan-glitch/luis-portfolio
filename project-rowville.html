Entertainment Unit Fireplace

Project Summary

From AI concept to architectural reality

This bespoke entertainment joinery project was developed from an AI-generated design concept provided by the client, which served as the foundation for a more refined and highly customised final outcome. The design was elevated through detailed craftsmanship, material refinement, and integrated functional solutions that transformed the original concept into a sophisticated architectural feature.

A key highlight is the incorporation of porcelain inlays throughout the shelving system, complemented by a striking porcelain centre column that adds depth, texture, and visual contrast. Custom powder-coated steel detailing was introduced through a purpose-built platform designed specifically to support the soundbar, enhancing both durability and the contemporary aesthetic.

To maintain a clean and seamless appearance, the television was engineered to function as a concealed access door, discreetly hiding a built-in cassette for storing the Apple TV and other media devices — keeping the space visually uncluttered while preserving full functionality.

Client Requirements

  • AI design concept as foundation
  • Porcelain inlays in shelving
  • Custom powder-coated steel soundbar platform
  • Concealed TV cassette for device storage
  • Integrated LED lighting throughout
  • Electric fireplace installation
