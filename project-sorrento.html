Sorrento Residence
House Lot & Bowling Alley

Mornington Peninsula

Bedroom Study Bowling Alley

Project Summary

Warmth, function & individuality

The custom-built residence in Sorrento was designed with a focus on creating a cohesive and refined interior language that balances warmth, functionality, and contemporary detailing. Beginning from an initial conceptual vision, the project evolved into a carefully curated collection of bespoke joinery elements that unify the home through warm timber tones, clean architectural lines, and understated detailing.

A defining feature of the project is the bowling alley-inspired cladding, which introduces a unique sense of character and individuality while maintaining a sophisticated architectural presence. Standout elements include the curved master bedroom bedhead, shark-nosed mitre detailing, and push-to-open drawer systems that contribute to a seamless and elegant finish.

The study area was crafted with durable 38mm shroud detailing, and in the main wardrobe, Coastal Oak internals combined with integrated LED lighting added warmth and depth to complement the home's material palette.

Client Requirements

  • Cohesive timber-look design language throughout
  • Polytec Coastal Oak finish to suit the home's palette
  • Moisture-resistant HMR carcasses where required
  • Curved master bedhead + shark-nosed mitres, PTO drawers
  • Study shrouds in 38mm panels with clean, solid detailing
  • Bowling alley wall + floor cladding as a feature element
