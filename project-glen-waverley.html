Custom Kitchen

Glen Waverley, VIC

Kitchen

Project Summary

Contemporary refinement, everyday practicality

This custom kitchen project was designed to deliver a contemporary and refined living space while maintaining the practicality required for everyday family use. The design focused on a clean and layered material palette, combining premium finishes with functional detailing to achieve a sophisticated yet highly usable environment.

The kitchen features 13mm Lavistone honed porcelain benchtops paired with custom 2-pack painted J-pull lower cabinetry and matte anti-fingerprint black upper cabinetry, creating a balanced contrast between warmth, texture, and modern minimalism.

A standout feature is the fluted glassware display cabinet, designed with smoked mirror backing and glass shelving to introduce elegance and visual interest. Integrated LED lighting was strategically incorporated throughout, while fully integrated appliances contribute to the seamless and uncluttered appearance.

Client Requirements

  • 13mm Lavistone honed porcelain benchtops
  • Custom 2-pack painted J-pull lower cabinetry
  • Matte anti-fingerprint black upper doors
  • Fluted glassware cabinet with smoked mirror backing
  • Integrated LED lighting throughout
  • Seamless appliance integration
  • Glass shelving in display cabinetry
