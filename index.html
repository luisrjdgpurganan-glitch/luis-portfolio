About

Design thinking,
built to last

I work across the full spectrum of architectural joinery — from initial concept and design development through to technical drafting, material specification, and production documentation. Every project I take on is treated as a holistic design problem, not just a cabinet-making exercise.

My background spans architectural design, joinery drafting, estimating, and material takeoff, giving me a complete picture of each project from both a design and a manufacturing perspective. I also develop workflow automations that streamline the production process and reduce errors on site.

All projects were undertaken as part of my role at Custom Fitted, Australia.
