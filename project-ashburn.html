Custom Office

Malvern East, VIC

Office

Project Summary

A modern workspace, precisely detailed

This bespoke office fit-out was designed for a residential space in Malvern East, focused on delivering a modern and functional workspace through custom-built joinery that combines practicality, storage efficiency, and contemporary aesthetics. The design features a full-height joinery system with integrated desk space, overhead wall cabinets, shelving, hanging storage, drawer units, and decorative panel detailing.

The joinery utilised a combination of white melamine carcasses, satin 2-pack finished MR MDF panels in Dark Newport Blue, and Polytec Boston Oak Woodmatt finishes to achieve a premium and sophisticated appearance. Custom shaker-profile doors and drawers, VJ panelling details, and recessed LED channel lighting were incorporated to enhance the visual appeal and overall user experience.

High-quality Titus Tekform hardware systems and adjustable legs were specified to ensure durability, smooth operation, and long-term performance across the full installation.

Client Requirements

  • Custom office joinery
  • Polytec Boston Oak desk top and floating shelf
  • Dark Newport Blue doors and panels
  • Hanging rods
  • Integrated LED lighting throughout
